About this site

This website is a demonstration site for the tutorial series "How to BUILD UP" on digital oceans
community platform. Learn how to recreate and customize this site with html by following the
step by step instructions in the tutorial series. participants will finish with an understanding of
HTML and a website ready to deploy to the cloud. No coding experience necessary!
